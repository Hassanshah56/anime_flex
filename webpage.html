Free guy

2021/PG-13/1h 55m

Free Guy (2021) is a sci-fi action-comedy film directed by Shawn Levy, starring Ryan Reynolds as Guy, a mild-mannered NPC (non-playable character) in an open-world video game called Free City. Guy's life is turned upside down when he begins to gain self-awareness and realizes he's part of a violent video game world. As he embarks on a journey to become the hero of his own story, Guy teams up with a human player named Millie (Jodie Comer) to stop the game's creators from shutting down Free City altogether. Blending elements of gaming culture, artificial intelligence, and virtual reality, Free Guy explores themes of identity, free will, and the impact of technology on human lives. The film is a fun, energetic mix of humor, action, and heart, offering a fresh take on the idea of characters in a digital world becoming more than just their programming. With Ryan Reynolds' comedic charm and a star-studded supporting cast, Free Guy is both a love letter to video game fans and a reflection on how games influence our sense of self and our connections to the world around us.
